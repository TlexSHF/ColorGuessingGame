Rules:


Hello and Welcome to the Color Game!
Upon starting the game, you will be given three difficulties.
Each difficulty gives a different set of points awarded for each correct answer.
When the difficulty is chosen, you will be provided with a big box,
and three smaller boxes colored in various colors.
The big box' color will be the main color.
Your goal is to click the small box with the corresponding color.
Each correct answer will give you some points based on your difficulty.
When you get multiple right answers in a row, you will get a streak.
The higher the streak is, the more points you will get from each correct answer.
The game will tell you if you chose correctly.
You can exit the game at any time.
Remember to write your name in the end to get your score on the scoreboard!
(Or you can go anonym. Your choice!)
